# Welcome to HugMeNow

Your emotional wellness platform for virtual support, mood tracking, and connecting with others.

## Features

- **Mood Tracking**: Record and visualize your emotional wellness journey
- **Virtual Hugs**: Send and receive support from your network
- **Community Connection**: Find supportive communities for shared experiences
- **Personalized Insights**: Gain insights about your emotional patterns

- [Track your mood](/mood-tracker)
- [Send a virtual hug](/hug-center)
